SEGODI (Software Engineering Guidelines for Outsourced Development and Integration)


To foster a dynamic marketplace where efficiency, affordability and tailored solutions converge, empowering both consumers and SMME business owners alike.

SEGODI is the new and big next thing. Enabling producers and consumers to easily interact with one another. This platform allows the consumers to charge their own prices for a specified service or product. Producers or providers of these services have the opportunity to accept, reject and negotiate orders of the given price. Its a connecting App for any service or product a consumer might require.


The following are just a few services that are offered by SEGODI:

  • Barbermen for your hair
  • Restaurants, both small and large Food Entreprises
  • Dry cleaners
  • Cabs services and many more..

SEGODI is an innovative platform revolutionizing how end consumers connect with product and service providers, akin to the seamless model of Uber. Designed as an intuititve app, SEGODI serves as the bridge between Small, Medium and Macro Entreprises (SMMEs) and the demand for their offerings. Here, users effortlessly present their specific needs or tasks, accompanied by a proposed price for resolution. On the flip side, product or service providers promptly respond to these offers, either accepting or declining based on their capacity and expertise.

THE AIMS AND INTENTIONS OF SEGODI ENTERPRISES:

  • Promote small and Medium businesses by giving them the opportunity to source clients through us.
  • Create a convienient enviromnent for the consumers as SEGODI will be delivering services to you, where ever!
  • Allow customers to charge thier preferred price for a particular service and also allow the providers of these
    to negotiate or accept if they are indeed satisfied with charged price.

We strive to provide the best customer service there is and for any queries and complaints the customer may have they should fill in the Customer complaints form and our Customer complaints handling team will appropiately handle the complaint.
